About Us

Share The Spare is a charity organization dedicated to helping those in need by connecting donors with recipients. We believe that even the smallest act of kindness can make a big difference.

Our mission is to create a community where everyone has the opportunity to share their excess resources with those who need them the most. Whether it's clothes, food, money, or other items, your donations can bring hope and happiness to many lives.
